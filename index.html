How to Play

1

Each player has 6 pieces in 3 sizes — small, medium, and large

2

Place pieces on the 3×3 board, or gobble smaller pieces by covering them with bigger ones

3

Line up 3 of your pieces in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win!
